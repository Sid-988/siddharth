Support & Help Center

How can I download the games I purchased?

Once you've purchased a game, go to your Library and click the "Download" button next to the title.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds can be requested within 14 days of purchase if the game has less than 2 hours of playtime. Contact us via the form below with your order ID.

I didn’t receive my game key. What should I do?

Check your email spam folder. If still not found, fill out the form below and we'll resend it manually.

Can I change the currency on the website?

Yes. Use the currency selector at the top right of the website to switch between INR and USD.
